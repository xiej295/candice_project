the 24 solar terms

Basic Introduction

The 24 solar terms, based on the sun's position in the zodiac, were created by farmers in ancient China to guide the agricultural affairs and farming activities. The 24 solar terms reflect the changes in climate, natural phenomena, agricultural production, and other aspects of human life, including clothing, food, housing, and transportation. The 24 solar terms play important roles and have greatly influenced people's basic needs in life, and they still have an important function nowadays.

  • climate changes
  • natural phenomena
  • agricultural affairs
  • aspects of human life
  • natural phenomena
  • plants

Magical Triangles

Basic Introduction

There are many kinds of triangles, such as right triangle, obtuse triangle, acute triangle. Different kinds of triangle can create different patterns. Unlike circle and rectangle, triangle could be really different with each others. The triangles drawn by each person will look different. When learning pencil sketch, the first thing to learn is perspective. Perspective has a strong connection with the triangle. Architects use triangles to create a sense of space.

  • different size
  • perspective
  • sense of space
  • constructivism
  • animation
